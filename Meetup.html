    Trek to the top, Jindhaghada the highest peak of Andhra Pradesh which is in the Araku Range....

    Trek Notes :

    Only for Ages 18+ and who are physically fit.

    Where :Jindhagada Peak, Araku valley

    Difficulty level : Medium,( People who are FIT can RSVP)

    Duration : Friday to Sunday (Back on Monday morning)

    ----------------------------------------------------------------

    Last year a few adventurers from the club, discovered and summitted the peak. And this year, yet again we aim to conquer the highest peak of Andhra Pradesh.

    The Eastern Ghats, are a discontinuous range of mountains along India's eastern coast. The Eastern Ghats run from West Bengal state in the north, through Orissa and Andhra Pradesh to Tamil Nadu in the south passing some parts of Karnataka.

    The Jindhagada Peak is the tallest peak of Eastern Ghats, located in the beautiful Araku Valley of Andhra Pradesh. Jindhagada Peak is also the highest mountain peak of Andhra Pradesh with an elevation of 1,690 m (5,540 ft). Araku Valley is well known for natural beauty and coffee plantation but the place is one of the least explore in India.

    Tentative Itinerary: [May vary ]

    Friday:

    Fri 5 pm : Sec-bad to Vizag train

    Saturday:

    Sat 6.30am: Take a Train from Vizag to araku & then to Base point

    Sat 12pm: Reach Base and start trekking

    Sat 7 pm: Reach the peak & prepare for the night stay. (We will carry tents to camp if its still raining or we will use tarps and enjoy the cold :) )

    Sat 8pm: Camp fire(subject to availability) & Pot luck Dinner

    Sat 9pm: Stargazing & Sentry duty allocations

    Sunday

    Sun 6am: Trek down to Base.

    Sun 1pm: Reach Araku, see some places if time permits.

    Sun 2pm: Lunch (some hot spicy bamboo chicken if time permits) and Start to Vizag

    Sun 8pm: Start to HYD from Vizag

    1) No smoking and no alcohol.

    ------------------------------------------------------------------

    Things to bring: [ All are must ] and wrap everything in polythene so that they do not get wet..

    1) A Rucksack / haversack, in other words a bag u can put all your stuff in and hang on ur back, try to keep the bag less then 5 kgs with 2 lts water
    2) Raincoat/poncho for yourself with rain cover for bag
    3) Trekking /sport shoes with extra pair of socks [Preferably shoes which cover till your ankle]
    4) Torch / Flashlight /headlight
    5) 1 track pants/ loose trousers - full length & a T Shirt and other Toiletries needed.
    6) Mosquito repellent.
    7) Toiletries required
    8) Sleeping bags or blankets are must.
    9) Photo Id (any valid card with your photograph and signature on it)

    * Great Hyderabad Adventure Club - GHAC is the Trekking and Adventure Club of Hyderabad and caters to Hiking, Trekking Adventure and Outdoor Adventure Activities all over India. we also conduct on Trekking, Hiking, Camping, Outdoors, Bird Watching and Adventure sports like Bouldering, Rock climbing, Rappelling, Paragliding and White Water Rafting in Rest of India.

    GHAC is registered with Govt of India as a Not for Profit Adventure Club and is open to all and run by trained and experienced professionals and volunteers.

    GHAC has been mentioned into the Limca Books of records -2013. The club is growing and is the most active adventure club in India by the sheer number of adventure activities the club does all round the year

    We have the following Divisions in GHAC catering to a core focus area.

    Adventure | Nature | Climbing & Mountaineering | Sports & Fitness | Travel

    * The Club Membership is free and open to everyone above 18 years. Young members above 13 years may participate with parental consent. Children above 6 and upto 12 years may participate along with parents only.

    * Safety is priority for us - Our Outdoor Leaders are trained and certified in their respective fields and know first aid and emergency handling. They have significant experience with outdoor activities and work very hard to make each event a safe and enjoyable experience.

    * Our Mission is to get people together for the purposes of:

    • Promote Adventure activities in Hyderabad and Andhra Pradesh.
    • Character Building, Leadership and Youth Development
    • Nature Study, Awareness, Care and Conservation.
    • Bring adventure activity to the poor and underprivileged children.
    • Provide assistance to the society during calamities.
